Performance design of a lunar habitat subjected to seismic loads and meteorite impacts.

A structure built on the lunar surface will be affected by multiple natural hazards due to the lack of atmospheric protection and the highly hostile environment. The speed at which these impacts occur can reach 80 km/s, this condition makes the experimental evaluation complex and a numerical simulation of the response (Figure 1) is chosen. These results together with the performance-based design philosophy allow the design of structures taking into account the probability of occurrence of impacts, guaranteeing the integrity of the structures and the safety of the crew inside the structure.
