Performance design of a lunar habitat subjected to seismic loading.

The design of lunar structures (Figure 1) must address the stochastic nature of lunar quakes and the uncertainty associated with the structural response to such loads. To achieve this, it is necessary to employ risk-based design methodologies that allow for an explicit evaluation of the performance of structures (Allende et al., 2021). In recent years, the performance-based design philosophy has been proposed for the design of lunar structures. However, there are currently no studies that apply performance-based design to lunar structures using nonlinear dynamic analysis with seismic demands derived from probabilistic seismic hazard studies.
